About Rate A Tradesman

Welcome to Rate A Tradesman, brought to you by The Weekly Tradesman, where skill meets fun! We've created a unique platform that brings a playful twist to the world of skilled trades. Our mission? To celebrate the hard work and expertise of tradespeople while adding a dash of friendly competition and camaraderie to the mix.

At The Weekly Tradesman, we believe that recognizing talent should be both informative and entertaining. That's why we've developed a system that allows you to rate your friends, co-workers, and employees across various skills and attributes. It's not just about finding the best tradesperson for the job – it's about fostering a community where skills are appreciated, improvements are encouraged, and a little friendly rivalry keeps everyone on their toes.

Whether you're a business owner looking to assess your team's strengths, a tradesperson curious about how you stack up against your peers, or simply someone who enjoys acknowledging the skills of others, Rate A Tradesman has something for you. Rate, compare, and discover top talent in a way that's engaging and fun.

So, dive in and start exploring! Rate your co-workers, check out how your team compares to others in the industry, or use our platform to find skilled tradespeople for your next project. At The Weekly Tradesman, we're turning skill assessment into a social experience that everyone can enjoy. Join us in celebrating the expertise that builds and maintains our world – one rating at a time!
